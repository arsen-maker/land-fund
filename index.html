Investment thesis

We invest where three forces converge.

01
GDP per capita growth

Rising income creates end-buyer demand. We target markets with sustained 4%+ growth trajectories — where the population can afford what gets built on the land.

02
Tourism expansion

External visitor demand reprices coastal and resort land independently of local income. Vietnam +19%, Georgia +7%, Saudi Arabia +8% — structural shifts, not cyclical bounces.

03
Infrastructure buildout

Airports, highways, and logistics hubs create a known future event. We map the pipeline and acquire before public pricing reflects delivery. Entry: 18–36 months before opening.
